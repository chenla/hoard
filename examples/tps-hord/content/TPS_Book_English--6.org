** Notes

The English translation by Productivity Press, 1988.
Translated for Western audiences during the period when
American manufacturers were trying to understand Japan's
manufacturing superiority.  This Expression made TPS
accessible to the English-speaking world and catalyzed
the lean manufacturing movement.
