** Notes

Based on the MIT 5-million-dollar, 5-year study on the
future of the automobile industry.  This book coined the
term "lean manufacturing" and introduced Toyota's
production methods to a Western audience.  It demonstrated
that TPS was not a culturally-bound Japanese phenomenon
but a universally applicable approach to manufacturing.
