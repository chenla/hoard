** Notes

Taiichi Ohno's definitive account of the Toyota Production
System, first published in Japanese in 1978 as トヨタ
生産方式.  Translated into English in 1988 by Productivity
Press.

This is the primary source for understanding TPS from the
perspective of its creator.  Ohno explains the system not
as a set of techniques but as a way of thinking about
production that starts from the customer and works backward.
